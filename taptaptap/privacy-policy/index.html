Privacy policy

Tap Tap Tap — Last updated: April 1, 2026

Tap Tap Tap is an educational game made by SMBCheeky SRL ("we", "us"). This policy covers what information the app collects and how we handle it.

What we collect

The app works offline. There are no accounts, no logins, and no personal information required.

A few things are saved on your device and never leave it: a display name (if entered), preferences like language and color theme, and which game was played last.

A random device ID is generated on first launch and sent to PostHog (hosted in the EU) so we can count how many devices use the app. PostHog receives this ID and nothing else.

If the app crashes, a report is sent to Sentry (also EU-hosted) with technical details about the crash. No personal information is included.

If you submit feedback through the app, the message and the device ID are sent to Sentry. We do not attach any name or contact details.

What we do not collect

  • Names, emails, or contact details (these never leave the device)
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Location or GPS data (geolocation is explicitly disabled)
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Browsing history or cross-app tracking
  • Usage patterns or behavioral data

Payments

The app is a paid download. All payments are handled by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not receive or store any payment or billing information. Refunds are also handled by the store where you purchased the app: Apple, Google Play.

Third-party services

PostHog (anonymous device counting, EU-hosted): privacy policy.

Sentry (crash reports and feedback, EU-hosted): privacy policy.

No data is shared with anyone else. There are no ads in the app.

Data retention

Local data stays on your device until you uninstall the app or clear its data. Sentry and PostHog retain data per their standard retention policies.

Data deletion

We do not hold personal data, so there is nothing to delete on our end. The device ID cannot be linked to anyone. Uninstalling the app removes all local data.

Your rights

If you have questions about your data, contact us at the email below. Since all identifiers are anonymous, we are not able to link stored data to any specific user.

Changes

If we update this policy, we will post the new version here with an updated date.
