Tap Tap Tap — Last updated: April 3, 2026

Some of the images and audio in Tap Tap Tap were generated with AI. This page covers what we used and under what terms.

Images

Illustrations in the app (animal outlines, character actions, geometric shapes, and other picture cards) were generated using image models on fal.ai. All models we used are marked "Commercial use" on their model pages.

Models used: FLUX 2 Pro (Black Forest Labs), Ideogram Character, BiRefNet, Bria RMBG, Recraft Upscale, Topaz Upscale, Nano Banana 2 (Google), SeedVR Upscale, Seedream 4.5 (ByteDance), and rembg-enhance (smoretalk-ai). We edited, resized, and optimized the generated images before shipping them in the app.

Audio

Voice recordings for letters, numbers, colors, words, and picture card labels were generated with the text-to-speech service on elevenlabs.io. All audio was generated from a paid subscription account using generally available (non-alpha, non-beta) services and the Eleven Multilingual v2 and Eleven v3 models. Under ElevenLabs' terms, content generated on a paid plan with non-beta services includes a commercial license.

No content is generated on your device

The app does not generate anything on your device. All AI-generated content was created during development and ships with the app.
